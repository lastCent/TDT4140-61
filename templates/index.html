Services

Providing a whole new academic experience

Smart learning

As as student, you couldnt really care less about doing your weekly exercises. But what if the exercises actually adopt to your knowledge and learning? Pineapple provides students with valuable insights, and guidelines.

Adaptive teaching

Professors all over the world have problems meeting understanding and meeting the individual students needs. With pineapple students and professors wil be tied closer, with less effort to ensure well suited teaching for everyone.

Big Picture

Pineapple provides the total picture of your courses. Either if you are a student, or a professor you can easily access valuable information about the class.

About

Solving real issues with new technology.

  • Our Pineapple Vision

    The future of education will be more resource-efficient and less time-consuming for both student and professor. Learning platforms will fit the individual students intellectual and academic level. This is Pineapple.

  • The mission

    By building pineapple, the team work to bridge the gap beetween todays conservative educational system and the future of academics. Pinapple is a digital platform thata will transform education for the better.

  • Digitisation For Insights

    With Pineapple students receive valuable information about what they know well. Furthermore insights about what you need to read more about, and where to find it saves time for both student and professor. To supplement this, we have added fuctionality to support total statistics for lecturers about their class performances.
