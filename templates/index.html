The Pineapple Vision

At pineapple we work everyday to enhance the way students learn, and teachers teach. The long overdue conservative ways of learning must be changed to enhance the student-lecturer comunication. The future of teaching integrates a digital learning assistant which understands your needs better than your own professor.

Our mission

By creating Pineapple the team work to bring the future to you. Pineapple will become the number one university platform for exercises, and automated student and teacher feedback. By this approach Pinepple will save both students, and universities valuable time and resources.
